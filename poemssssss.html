" THE VOYAGER "



I

It was all a world of games and endless days ,

wandering through life's modest maze ,

The fields looked wide , so the mind was ,

The heart was free , so the passion was .

Hath aspiring dreams beneath the old oak tree ,

" Maybe some fulfilled , and some yet to be !! "

" Days went like bare thoughts do these days "...

Hath chased the infinite clouds but toppled on stone,

"Atleast, hath a heart untouched by love's unknowns"



II

Thy soul was aware of what thy heart needed ,

Maybe was craving for the love's tender plea

But thy restless heart doth never to be deceived ,

Kept wandering free , through dreams and reality...

" The search for connection subdued ,

But thy heart remained distant ,

untouched by love's glow ,

Embracing the freedom that only few know

Marking thy restless heart solemnly thine ".



III

For years do pass like fleeting seasons do ,

Never realised the phase which I was going through .

Just the heart , marked thine ,

Just the passion , as fine as Italian wine .

With burden of everlasting thoughts ,

with expectations hum along .

Spilling out the spell of intense worry ,

Like how corrosive caustics do bury .

Felt the intensity of chores ,

Lost the Peace of Mind , no more ..

But failed to persuade thy entire soul convincing :

“ that Physics need not grace the chef's domain,

Nor must chemistry adorn the athlete's refrain.”

But the wisdom of intwined discipline still remained,

Yet , truth kept defying the freedom unchained.



IV

With thine worthy passion , waning away ,

I yearned even more for the ties of love , multiplying.

Soon , amid god's mysteries , love I did see ,

A testament divine , unfolding for thee .

Gazes locked , conveying gossips of the lost star ,

" Helix Nebula speaking volumes ,

beyond mere words can ever last ."



V

For I mistook love to be a fleeting star ,

As rare as Halley's comet in the sky afar .

Enthralled by the spring's floral array ,

When thy love for u didn't sway .

For in the season of bloom ,

I longed to claim you mine .

I always stared at you with thy solstice eyes ,

Carving for those sophisticated looks of glory.

We vowed to weave dreams from Stardust and light

But the worthiest one was :

“ will you forever be mine ? ” ...



VI

A dream , doth serene and bright ,

As we basked together in the summer's golden light..

Shared a mystic chemistry , volatile and free ,

While our love surged like a storm upon the Sea...

In the season of love , as we felt petrichor's embrace,

Dancing in the very first rain ,

we found our perfect place...



VII

But With the very onset of leaves falling down,

Our love began to wane , losing its crown.

The crisp air spoke of change, so bold and cold,

In the fading autumn, our story lost its hold.

As winter's chill arrived with frosty grace ,

Our hearts grew more and more distant ,

No more touch to veil , no more warmth to hail .



VIII

It all ended before the faded leaves could ever revive

As Autumn's soft breath whispered low ,

Promises , like sand , began to go .

Fading with leaves in a gentle sigh ,

Trust and hope drifted , just left to die....

In the chill of Twilight , where our memories do lie,

We bid our last goodbyes beneath the murky , brisk sky...



IX

Returned to thy worthy fervor ,

the one that faded long ago ,

For in the search of Redamancy , all I found

were emotions performing an act of diolomacy..

But perhaps the weight of time and strain ,

Collected broken pieces, mending the pain.

Turning those shards into Kintsugi's art,

Storing memories, a hopeful heart.



X

Now I've lost hope for the passion that's dead ,

And become an Amorist , where thy heart is led ...

Turning through the pages of thy heart’s own twist,

Seeking a chapter that's lost, to find its end ,

For poetry’s end is a journey to amend .



But how can I end it, when the start's unclear?

The story's beginning is what I still fear...
