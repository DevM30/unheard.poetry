HARMONIC CHAOS



Beneath the bare trees ,

When the frosty veils sighed down the shining breeze ,

Spring brought back the embrace ,

following the enduring crystal spell ,

Emerging life through its radiant solace ...

Yet imperfection weaves life's fabric ,

threatening the season of bloom with disease whispering from nature's womb .

anticipating the forthcoming summer's warmth .



The scorching heat brings down the monsoon's allure ,

Emerging the season of love amidst the radiant valor .

While the mountains sigh the Charisma of emotions ,

witnessing the love's potion flowing alike eternal ocean .

As rain kisses the earth with gentle passion ,

giving a mild hint to the heartfelt prayers , Ensuring their way they forged ahead with dormant devotion ,

lifting up the spirits akin to petrichor's motion .
