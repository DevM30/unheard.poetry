ECHOES OF REMEMBRANCE



Amidst the clouds dwelling by the endless sight,

I felt hopeless when nothing sighed.

Drowning at the very edge of doubt,

Overwhelmed by the promises thou hath made.

Hoping for those to come to forsake me ...

Felt a lot of disgust ,

like the ocean felt after ensuring it's every breath to the shore ,

But never knew about the embrace the shore hath provided .



By the dusk, when I felt lonely,

I always remember the path we hath shared long ago,

Getting those sleepless nights back again

But by the dawn, whenever I find a ray of light,

a ray of enthusiasm,

Witnessing the peace of the everlasting colors of love,

I always find a hope :

" Not the hope of meeting those memories again ".

" But the prospect of forgetting those forever " .
